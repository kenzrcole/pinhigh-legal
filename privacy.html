Privacy Policy

PinHigh · Last updated: March 27, 2026

PinHigh (“we,” “us,” “our”) provides golf-related software and services, including mobile and web experiences. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use PinHigh.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data. If you create an account (e.g. email, display name), we store what you provide and data needed to operate authentication.
  • Location. With your permission, we may access device location to show on-course features (e.g. yardages, map). You can revoke location access in your device settings.
  • Usage & diagnostics. We may collect technical data (device type, app version, crash logs) to improve reliability and performance.
  • Payments. Purchases may be processed by Stripe or other processors. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers; payment providers handle card data under their own policies.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve PinHigh features.
  • Authenticate users, sync preferences, and deliver subscriptions or entitlements where applicable.
  • Respond to support requests and comply with law.

3. Sharing

We may share information with service providers who help us run the product (e.g. hosting, authentication, maps, analytics, payments), subject to appropriate safeguards. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account where applicable; some records may be retained as required by law.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children

PinHigh is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. International users

If you use PinHigh from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S. or other countries where we or our providers operate.

8. Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. Contact us using the details below to make a request.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date.
